Want to know about us ?

This is GyanJyoti, an AI Enabled E-Learning Platform Developed as part of a project Funded by Assam Science, Technology & Environment Council (ASTEC).

The main aim of GyanJyoti is to provide customized and flexible Learning experince to the people from different countries across the globe with the assitance of Artificial Intellegence. For that purpose, people can register in different courses of their interest and learn at their own Pace
